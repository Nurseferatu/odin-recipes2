White Rice Recipe

Ingedients

  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon NaCl
  • 1 tablespoon buttah

Instructions

  • 1. Rince long grain white rice in collander until water runoff is clear
  • 2. Drain rice, pour into pot, add the rest of ingredient
  • 3. Bring water to boil, reduce to simmer, and cover pot
  • 4. Remove from heat at 17 minutes*
  • 5. Let sit for 5 minutes while remaining covered
  • 6. fluff rice with a fork and sit for 2 minutes before serving
  • * may require up to 5 minutes more time if cooking larger quantities; check rice with fork

Note:

When using a ricecooker, the ingredients ratios do not change, but follow manufacturer recommendations for cook times
