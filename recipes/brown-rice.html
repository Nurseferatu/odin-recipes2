Brown Rice Recipe

Brown Rice Cooking Directions

Ingedients

  • 1 cup uncooked brown rice, rinsed well
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil

Instructions

  • 1. Combine igredients in a 4 quart pot and bring to a rolling boil.
  • 2. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 45 minutes
  • 3. Remove from heat and let sit, while covered, for an additional 10 minutes

Note:

If using a rice cooker, use the brown rice setting with the same ingredient ratios.
